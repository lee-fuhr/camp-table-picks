How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests. Instead, we pore over long‑term owner reviews, published reviews from outlets with rigorous standards, and repair forums to see what actually holds up. The most common failure points – leg hinges that loosen, carrying bags that split after a season, and tabletops that warp in direct sun – become deal‑breakers unless a model has a proven track record of avoiding them.
